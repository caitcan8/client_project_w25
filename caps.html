Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

At the University of Michigan, we understand that college life is as challenging as it is rewarding. CAPS is here to support you through the ups and downs of your academic and personal journey. Our mission is to foster the psychological well-being and academic success of our diverse student body by providing an array of mental health services.

Our Services

Individual Counseling

Work one-on-one with a skilled counselor to explore personal issues, manage stress, and navigate life changes.

Group Therapy

Connect with peers facing similar challenges in a supportive, therapist-led environment. Group topics range from anxiety management to cultural adjustment.

Workshops and Outreach

Attend workshops designed to teach coping skills, mindfulness, and other techniques to enhance emotional resilience and overall wellness.

Crisis Support

Access emergency consultation services if you or someone you know is in immediate distress.

Contact Us!

  • Call Us: Reach out to our office at (734) 764-8312 to speak with a counselor.
  • Walk-In: Visit our central office located on the 4th floor of the Michigan Union for a quick consultation and to learn about your options
  • Online Scheduling: Use our online portal to book an appointment at your convenience.
